Passage Island (Duncan Island)

Passage Island, also known as Duncan Island, is an uninhabited island of the Andaman Islands, located in the Bay of Bengal. It is part of the South Andaman administrative district in the Indian union territory of Andaman and Nicobar Islands. The island lies approximately 53 km (33 mi) south of Port Blair.

Passage Island is part of the Rutland Archipelago, situated between South Cinque Island (6.2 km to the north) and West Sister Island (6.4 km to the southeast). The Duncan Passage lies just south of the island, separating it from Little Andaman Island.
