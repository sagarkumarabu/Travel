Aerial Bay - Overview

Administrative Details

  • Country: India
  • Union Territory: Andaman and Nicobar Islands
  • District: North and Middle Andaman
  • Nearest Town: Diglipur
  • PIN Code: 744202
  • Telephone Code: 03192
  • Time Zone: UTC+5:30 (IST)

Geographical Features

  • Location: Northern tip of Middle Andaman Island
  • Bay Type: Natural Harbour
  • Elevation: Sea Level
  • Nearest Airport: Veer Savarkar Airport, Port Blair (approx. 300 km)
  • Coordinates: Approx. 13.25°N, 93.00°E

Significance

Aerial Bay is known for its natural harbour, lush tropical surroundings, and proximity to eco-tourism spots. It serves as a key marine connection for northern Andaman islands, including Smith and Ross Islands.

The bay is also a small port area with ferry access and is part of the Indian Coast Guard’s regional operations.

Nearby Attractions

  • Saddle Peak: The highest point in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands (732 m)
  • Kalipur Beach: Known for turtle nesting and black volcanic sand
  • Lamiya Bay: A serene beach ideal for sunset views
  • Alfred Caves: Limestone cave system near Diglipur
  • Ross and Smith Islands: Twin islands connected by a sandbar, accessible from Aerial Bay Jetty
