Geography

Ooty is located in the Nilgiri Hills, part of the Western Ghats within the Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve. It is bordered by the Moyar River to the north (separating it from Karnataka) and the Palghat Gap to the south, separating it from the Anaimalai and Palani Hills.

The town is situated at an altitude of 2,240 metres above sea level and covers a total area of 30.36 km².

Nearby Natural Attractions

  • Doddabetta Peak: The highest peak in the Nilgiris (2,623 m), located 10 km from Ooty.
  • Ooty Lake: A 65-acre artificial lake created in 1824.
  • Pykara River & Falls: Located 19 km from Ooty, with cascades including 55 m and 61 m tall falls.
  • Kamaraj Sagar Dam: Located 10 km from Ooty.
  • Other Lakes: Emerald Lake, Avalanche Lake, and Porthimund Lake.
